About RedRoute

Scissors is a easy-to-use tool that allows you to shorten long URLs.

Red Route is perfect for:
- Sharing links on social media
- Sending links in emails
- Including links in your website or blog

Red Route is also a great way to track clicks on your links. With Red Route, you can see how many people have clicked on your links and where they are coming from.
To use Red Route, simply enter the long URL in the box and click on the "Shorten URL" button. Red Route will then generate a short, unique URL for you. You can then copy and paste the short URL wherever you want to share it.
Red Route is a great way to make your links more shareable and trackable. Sign up today and start shortening your links!
