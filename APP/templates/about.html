About Scissors

Scissors is a free and easy-to-use tool that allows you to shorten long URLs.

Scissors is a great way to track clicks on your links.
To use Scissors, simply enter the long URL in the box and click on the "Shorten URL" button. scissors will then generate a short, unique URL for you. which you can then copy and paste the short URL wherever you want to share it.
